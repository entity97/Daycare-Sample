Our Programs

Infant Care (6 weeks - 12 months)

Our infant program provides nurturing care in a safe, stimulating environment. Our experienced caregivers focus on:

  • Individual attention and care
  • Age-appropriate activities
  • Motor skill development
  • Regular communication with parents

Toddler Program (1-2 years)

Our toddler program encourages exploration and early learning through:

  • Interactive play
  • Basic language development
  • Social skills
  • Creative activities

Preschool (3-4 years)

Our preschool program prepares children for kindergarten with:

  • Early literacy and numeracy
  • Arts and crafts
  • Music and movement
  • Science exploration

Pre-K (4-5 years)

Our Pre-K program focuses on kindergarten readiness through:

  • Advanced cognitive development
  • Writing and reading preparation
  • Mathematical concepts
  • Social and emotional development
